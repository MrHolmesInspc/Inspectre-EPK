About Inspectre

Inspectre is a Berlin-based alternative rock band blending post-grunge intensity with melodic sophistication. The lineup — Chris (vocals), Shaun (guitar), Harsha (bass), and Pascal (drums) — crafts anthems that are both raw and cinematic.

Their latest single "Full Moon" has garnered praise for its energy and genre-bending power. With powerful live shows and a unique sound, Inspectre is becoming a must-see act in Berlin and beyond.
